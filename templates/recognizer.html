How Automatic Camera Control Works:

  • Step 1: Click "Start Camera" to begin face verification for {{ username }}
  • Step 2: Position your face clearly in front of the camera
  • Step 3: The system verifies your face against trained data (requires 3 consecutive matches)
  • SUCCESS SCENARIO: If your face matches the trained data:
    • Attendance is automatically recorded
    • Camera stops automatically
    • Success message is displayed
  • MISMATCH SCENARIO: If a different face is detected:
    • System counts consecutive mismatches (up to 5)
    • After 5 mismatches, camera stops automatically
    • NO attendance is recorded
    • Error message shows which face was detected
  • Anti-duplicate: Each person can only mark attendance once per minute
  • View Results: Check "Today's Attendance" page to see recorded entries
